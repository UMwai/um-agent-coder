## 2026-01-30 - [CLI Approval Clarity]
**Learning:** Dense text blocks in critical decision prompts (like plan approval) increase cognitive load and risk blindness. Using color-coded metrics (Red for high complexity/risk, Green for low cost) and visual hierarchy (Bold headers) helps users scan and make safer decisions faster.
**Action:** Always structure interactive CLI confirmations with clear visual hierarchy and semantic coloring for key metrics.
